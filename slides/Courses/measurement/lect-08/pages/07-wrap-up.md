# Recap

- Group membership is often really interesting to social scientists
- Sometimes we don't know what the groups should be in advance
- Clustering allows us to uncover latent group membership
- We can describe clusters the same way we describe components, factors, or topics
- We can also cluster on a subset of variables and then look at distributions of other variables within clusters
