# Recap

<v-clicks depth="2">

- GLMs rely on exponential family outcome distributions
- MLE estimates parameters by maximizing the likelihood, or equivalently the log likelihood
- For Bernoulli data, the MLE of $p$ is the sample proportion
- Binary outcomes can be modeled with logit, probit, or complementary log-log links
- Model evaluation depends on the comparison you care about: fit, parsimony, or classification performance

</v-clicks>
